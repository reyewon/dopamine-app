Pixieset Bookmarklet

Invoice Scraper for Pixieset Studio

Drag the purple button below to your bookmarks bar:
Sync Pixieset Invoices

How to use

1

Drag the purple button above to your bookmarks bar. Don't click it here (it won't work on this page).

2

Go to studio.pixieset.com/invoices and make sure your invoices have loaded.

3

Click the bookmarklet in your bookmarks bar. You will see a green notification confirming how many invoices were scraped and synced.

4

If you have multiple pages of invoices, navigate to each page and click the bookmarklet again.

If you can't see your bookmarks bar, press Cmd+Shift+B (Mac) or Ctrl+Shift+B (Windows/Linux).
